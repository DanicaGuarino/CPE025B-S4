Anya Forger

Anya was originally known as Test Subject "007" and was created as a result of experiments conducted by an unknown organization. She possesses telepathic abilities, allowing her to read minds. After escaping from the research facility, she bounced between orphanages before being adopted by Loid Forger. Anya's past is marked by her experiences in various institutions, where she developed street smarts and a survival instinct unusual for someone her age.
